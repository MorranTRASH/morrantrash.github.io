Organized & Professional

We have been here before and we know how it is done. With a Naxxramas experienced raidleader, <TRASH> understands the importance of preparation and communication. We offer you a smooth and enjoyable raiding experience where we take raiding seriously but ourselves not quite as much.

Player over Class

In <TRASH> we believe in the player over the class. We don't need the absolutely perfect setup in order to succeed as long as we have the right players. In order to do so, we have very high standards for all of our recruits.

It's OK to have a life

<TRASH> was created to bridge the gap between casual and hardcore in order to take on the best aspects of both styles. Through rigorous standards on new recruits and experience together with preparation we strive to keep our costs to the absolute minimum. You don't have to farm exalted with some faction to get a minor upgrade. You don't have to show up with flasks every raid. You are entirely free to dictate how much time you are willing to invest outside of raiding.
